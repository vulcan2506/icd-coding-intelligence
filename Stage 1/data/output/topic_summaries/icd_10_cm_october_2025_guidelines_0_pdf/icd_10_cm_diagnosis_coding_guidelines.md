# ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Chief and Coexisting Diagnosis Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- List the ICD-10-CM code for the diagnosis chiefly responsible for services provided first.
- List additional codes to describe any coexisting conditions.
- The first-listed diagnosis may be a symptom if a confirmed diagnosis has not been established by the provider.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The first-listed diagnosis must be the condition chiefly responsible for the services provided.
- Additional codes must describe coexisting conditions.
