# Outpatient ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Outpatient ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Guidelines in Section I (Conventions, general, and chapter-specific) must be applied to outpatient services and office visits.
- The terms 'encounter' and 'visit' are used interchangeably to describe outpatient service contacts.
- Coding guidelines for outpatient and provider reporting vary from inpatient diagnoses in specific instances.
- The UHDDS definition of principal diagnosis does not apply to hospital-based outpatient services or provider-based office visits.
- Coding guidelines for inconclusive diagnoses (probable, suspected, rule out) do not apply to outpatients.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Apply Section I conventions, general guidelines, and chapter-specific guidelines to outpatient services.
- Do not apply the UHDDS definition of principal diagnosis to outpatient settings.
- Do not code inconclusive diagnoses (probable, suspected, rule out) for outpatients.
