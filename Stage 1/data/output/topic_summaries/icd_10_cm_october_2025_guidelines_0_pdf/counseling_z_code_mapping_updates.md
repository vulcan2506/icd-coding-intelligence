# Counseling Z Code Mapping Updates
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Counseling Z Code Mapping Updates

**Key Behaviors:**
- Counseling Z codes are used when a patient or family member receives assistance in the aftermath of an illness or injury, or when support is required in coping with family or social problems.
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception is mapped to Encounter for procreative genetic counseling (Z31.5).
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception is mapped to Encounter for general counseling and advice on procreation (Z31.6-).
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception is mapped to Encounter for childbirth instruction (Z32.2).
- Additional mapping table rows exist for other counseling categories.
- The system distinguishes between general health counseling (Z71) and specific travel risk counseling (Z71.84) to ensure accurate coding for future travel purposes.
- Encounters involving mental health services for victims and perpetrators of abuse are mapped under contraception counseling codes, treating them as a unified counseling session.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Patient or family member must receive assistance in the aftermath of an illness or injury.
- Support must be required in coping with family or social problems.
- Documentation must specify the type of counseling (e.g., contraception, procreation, childbirth instruction).
- General counseling and advice on contraception must be mapped to the specific procreation-related Z code category.
- Encounter must be classified under the appropriate Z30-Z39 counseling category.
