# Counseling Z Code Mapping Updates
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Counseling Z Code Mapping Updates

**Key Behaviors:**
- Counseling Z codes are used when a patient or family member receives assistance in the aftermath of an illness or injury, or when support is required in coping with family or social problems.
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception (Z30.0-) is mapped to Encounter for procreative genetic counseling (Z31.5).
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception (Z30.0-) is mapped to Encounter for general counseling and advice on procreation (Z31.6-).
- Encounter for general counseling and advice on contraception (Z30.0-) is mapped to Encounter for childbirth instruction (Z32.2).
- Additional table rows exist for further counseling Z code mappings.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Patient or family member must receive assistance in the aftermath of an illness or injury.
- Support must be required in coping with family or social problems.
- Documentation must specify the type of counseling provided to match the correct Z code category.
- The encounter must be for general counseling and advice on contraception to utilize the specific mappings listed.
- The encounter must be for procreative genetic counseling or childbirth instruction as per the new mapping rules.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using Z30.0- for encounters that are specifically procreative genetic counseling without the new mapping.
- Using Z30.0- for encounters that are specifically general counseling and advice on procreation without the new mapping.
- Using Z30.0- for encounters that are specifically childbirth instruction without the new mapping.
- Failing to update coding practices to reflect the new Z code mappings for counseling encounters.
- Assigning unspecified counseling codes when specific counseling Z codes are now available and applicable.

**New in this version:**
- New mapping from Z30.0- to Z31.5 for procreative genetic counseling.
- New mapping from Z30.0- to Z31.6- for general counseling and advice on procreation.
- New mapping from Z30.0- to Z32.2 for childbirth instruction.
- Additional table rows for further counseling Z code mappings.
- Updated guidelines for assigning counseling Z codes based on specific counseling types.
