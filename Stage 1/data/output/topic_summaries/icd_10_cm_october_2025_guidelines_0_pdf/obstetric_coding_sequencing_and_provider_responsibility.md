# Obstetric Coding Sequencing and Provider Responsibility
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Obstetric Coding Sequencing and Provider Responsibility

**Key Behaviors:**
- Chapter 15 codes (O00-O9A) have sequencing priority over codes from other chapters.
- Additional codes from other chapters may be used in conjunction with chapter 15 codes to specify conditions.
- Code Z33.1 is used in place of chapter 15 codes if the pregnancy is incidental to the encounter.
- Providers are responsible for documenting that the condition being treated is not affecting the pregnancy.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be documented as affecting the pregnancy to use Chapter 15 codes.
- Provider must explicitly state the condition is not affecting the pregnancy to use Z33.1.
