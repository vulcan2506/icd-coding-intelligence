# Post-COVID-19 Follow-Up Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Post-COVID-19 Follow-Up Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign code Z09 for encounter for follow-up examination after completed treatment for conditions other than malignant neoplasm
- Assign code Z86.16 for personal history of COVID-19 when no residual symptoms or conditions exist
- Do not assign COVID-19 specific codes if the patient has no residual symptoms or conditions and test results are negative
- Refer to guideline I.C.1.g.1.m for follow-up visits involving individuals with symptom(s) or condition(s) related to previous COVID-19 infection
- Reference Section I.C.21.c.8 regarding factors influencing health states and contact with health services for follow-up

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- COVID-19 test results must be negative to assign Z09 and Z86.16 for asymptomatic follow-up
- Documentation must confirm absence of residual symptoms or conditions to apply the personal history code
- Clinicians must distinguish between asymptomatic follow-up and visits for post-COVID-19 symptoms to select the correct guideline
