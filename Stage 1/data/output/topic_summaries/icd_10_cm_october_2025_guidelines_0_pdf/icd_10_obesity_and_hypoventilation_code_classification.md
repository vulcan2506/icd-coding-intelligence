# ICD-10 Obesity and Hypoventilation Code Classification
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Obesity and Hypoventilation Code Classification

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in category E66 include specific codes for the cause of obesity, such as drug-induced obesity (E66.1)
- Codes in category E66 include specific codes for effects of obesity, such as Morbid (severe) obesity with alveolar hypoventilation (E66.2)
- Obesity codes exist in other categories for specific etiologies, such as Obesity due to disruption of MC4R pathway (E88.82)
- Obesity codes exist in other categories for specific complications, such as Obesity complicating pregnancy, childbirth, and the puerperium (O99.21)
- Classification distinguishes between codes related to the cause of obesity and codes related to the effects of obesity

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must specify the cause of obesity to assign codes like E66.1
- Documentation must specify the effects of obesity to assign codes like E66.2
- Documentation must identify specific etiologies like MC4R pathway disruption for codes like E88.82
- Documentation must identify pregnancy-related complications for codes like O99.21
