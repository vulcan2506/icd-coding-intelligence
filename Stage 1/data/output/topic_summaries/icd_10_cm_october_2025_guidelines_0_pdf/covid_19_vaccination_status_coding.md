# COVID-19 Vaccination Status Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** COVID-19 Vaccination Status Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign Z28.310 when the patient has not received a COVID-19 vaccine of any type.
- Assign Z28.311 when the patient has been partially vaccinated for COVID-19 as per CDC recommendations in place at the time of the encounter.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Vaccination status must align with CDC recommendations current at the time of the encounter.
- Documentation must explicitly indicate whether the patient is unvaccinated or partially vaccinated.
- Documentation must be provided by clinicians other than the patient's provider for underimmunization status.
- Refer to Section I.B.14 for specific documentation requirements regarding underimmunization.
