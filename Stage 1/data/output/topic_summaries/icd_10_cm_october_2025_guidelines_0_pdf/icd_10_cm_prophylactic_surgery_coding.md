# ICD-10-CM Prophylactic Surgery Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Prophylactic Surgery Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign principal code from subcategory Z40.0 or Z40.8 for encounters specifically for prophylactic organ removal due to risk factors.
- Assign additional codes to identify associated risk factors such as genetic susceptibility or family history.
- Assign a code for an existing malignancy at one site in addition to a Z40.0 code when prophylactic removal is performed at another site to prevent new primary or metastatic disease.
- Do not assign a Z40.0 code if the organ removal is for the treatment of an existing malignancy.
- Ensure the encounter is specifically for prophylactic removal, not therapeutic treatment of a known cancer.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must confirm the encounter is specifically for prophylactic removal of an organ.
- Documentation must identify the specific risk factor (e.g., genetic susceptibility, family history) if applicable.
- Documentation must distinguish between prophylactic removal and removal for treatment of an existing malignancy.
- If a malignancy exists at a different site, documentation must support the intent to prevent new primary or metastatic disease.
- The principal diagnosis must be selected from subcategory Z40.0 or Z40.8 based on the specific prophylactic procedure.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Assigning a Z40.0 code when the organ removal is for the treatment of a malignancy.
- Failing to assign additional codes for associated risk factors when present.
- Coding prophylactic surgery as a complication or sequela of a malignancy rather than a distinct encounter.
- Using unspecified codes for prophylactic surgery without specifying the risk factor or organ.
- Omitting the code for an existing malignancy when prophylactic surgery is performed at a different site.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit requirement to assign additional codes for associated risk factors like genetic susceptibility or family history.
- Clarification that Z40.0 codes are not to be used for therapeutic organ removal related to existing malignancies.
- Mandatory inclusion of a code for an existing malignancy when prophylactic surgery is performed at a different site to prevent metastasis.
- Specific guidance to use subcategory Z40.0 for risk factors related to malignant neoplasms and Z40.8 for other prophylactic surgeries.
- Emphasis on distinguishing prophylactic encounters from therapeutic encounters in the principal diagnosis selection.
