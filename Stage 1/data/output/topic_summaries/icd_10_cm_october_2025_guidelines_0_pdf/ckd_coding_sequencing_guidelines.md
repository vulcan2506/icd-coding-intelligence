# CKD Coding Sequencing Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** CKD Coding Sequencing and Contributing Conditions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Sequencing of CKD codes relative to other contributing conditions is determined by Tabular List conventions.
- Diabetes mellitus and hypertension are identified as common serious conditions affecting patients with CKD.
- Specific guidance is referenced for hypertensive chronic kidney disease (I.C.9).
- Specific guidance is referenced for chronic kidney disease and kidney transplant complications (I.C.19).

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must support the relationship between CKD and other contributing conditions.
- Coding must adhere to the specific conventions outlined in the ICD-10-CM Tabular List.
- Reference to specific sections (I.C.9, I.C.19) is required for hypertensive and transplant-related CKD cases.
