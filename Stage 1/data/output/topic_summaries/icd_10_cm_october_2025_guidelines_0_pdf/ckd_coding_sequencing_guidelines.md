# CKD Coding Sequencing Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** CKD Coding Sequencing with Contributing Conditions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Sequencing of CKD codes relative to other contributing conditions is determined by Tabular List conventions.
- Hypertensive chronic kidney disease requires specific reference to I.C.9 guidelines.
- Chronic kidney disease and kidney transplant complications require specific reference to I.C.19 guidelines.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must identify CKD alongside other serious conditions such as diabetes mellitus or hypertension.
- Coding must adhere to the specific conventions outlined in the ICD-10-CM Tabular List for sequencing.
- Specific I.C.9 guidelines must be consulted for hypertensive chronic kidney disease cases.
- Specific I.C.19 guidelines must be consulted for chronic kidney disease and kidney transplant complications.
