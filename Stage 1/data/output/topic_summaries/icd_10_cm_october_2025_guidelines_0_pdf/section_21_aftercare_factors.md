# Section 21 Aftercare Factors
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Section 21 Aftercare Factors

**Key Behaviors:**
- Factors influencing health status and contact with health services are categorized under Section I.C.21
- Aftercare codes are used to document ongoing treatment and monitoring following an initial encounter
- These factors influence health status and the frequency of contact with health services

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must clearly indicate the aftercare nature of the service provided
- The encounter must be for ongoing treatment or monitoring related to a previous condition or procedure
