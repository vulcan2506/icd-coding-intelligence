# Encounter Codes for Abnormal Findings
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Encounter Codes for Abnormal Findings

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign code for general medical examination with abnormal findings as the first-listed diagnosis if an abnormal finding is identified.
- An examination with abnormal findings refers to a newly identified condition or a change in severity of a chronic condition during a routine physical examination.
- A secondary code for the specific abnormal finding must also be coded.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The abnormal finding must be newly identified or represent a change in severity of a chronic condition.
- The finding must occur during a routine general medical examination or routine child health examination.
- The code for the examination with abnormal findings must be assigned as the first-listed diagnosis.
