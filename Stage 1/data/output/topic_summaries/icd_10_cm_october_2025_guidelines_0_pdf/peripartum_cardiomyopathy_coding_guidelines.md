# Peripartum Cardiomyopathy Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Peripartum Cardiomyopathy Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code O90.3 is used for cardiomyopathy diagnosed in the third trimester or progressing months after delivery.
- The condition must develop as a result of pregnancy.
- The patient must not have pre-existing heart disease.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Diagnosis must occur in the third trimester of pregnancy or continue to progress months after delivery.
- Cardiomyopathy must be a result of pregnancy.
- Patient must have no pre-existing heart disease.
