# ICD-10 Category M81 Osteoporosis Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Category M81 Osteoporosis Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Osteoporosis is treated as a systemic condition where site is not a component of codes under category M81.
- Site codes under category M80 identify the fracture site, not the osteoporosis itself.
- Category M81 is used for patients with osteoporosis who do not currently have a pathologic fracture due to the condition.
- Patients with a history of osteoporosis fractures must be assigned status code Z87.310 following the M81 code.
- M81 codes apply even if the patient has had a fracture in the past, provided the current condition is without a pathologic fracture.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must confirm the absence of a current pathological fracture to utilize category M81.
- Status code Z87.310 is mandatory for patients with a history of healed osteoporosis fractures.
- Site specification is not required for M81 codes as the condition is systemic.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Assigning site-specific codes to osteoporosis under category M81.
- Using M81 codes for patients with a current pathological fracture due to osteoporosis.

**New in this version:**
- Mandatory sequencing of status code Z87.310 after M81 codes for patients with a history of fractures.
