# Drug and Chemical Poisoning Coding Rules
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Drug and Chemical Poisoning Coding Rules

**Key Behaviors:**
- Do not code directly from the Table of Drugs and Chemicals; always refer back to the Tabular List for final code assignment.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Directly assigning codes from the Table of Drugs and Chemicals without verifying against the Tabular List.

**New in this version:**
- Mandatory cross-referencing of the Tabular List to ensure accurate classification of poisoning codes.
