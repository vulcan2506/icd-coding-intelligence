# Malignancy Diagnosis Coding Rules
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Malignancy Diagnosis Coding Rules

**Key Behaviors:**
- Designate primary malignancy or appropriate metastatic site as principal diagnosis when reason for admission is to determine extent of malignancy
- Designate primary malignancy or appropriate metastatic site as principal diagnosis for procedures such as paracentesis or thoracentesis
- Apply principal diagnosis designation even when chemotherapy or radiotherapy is administered

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Reason for admission/encounter must be to determine extent of malignancy
- Procedure must be paracentesis or thoracentesis
- Primary malignancy or appropriate metastatic site must be identified
