# Borderline Diagnosis Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Borderline Diagnosis Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Borderline diagnoses are coded as confirmed unless a specific ICD-10-CM entry exists for the condition
- Conditions with specific index entries in ICD-10-CM are coded using those specific entries
- No distinction is made between inpatient and outpatient care settings for borderline conditions
- Coders are encouraged to query for clarification when documentation regarding borderline conditions is unclear
- Borderline conditions are treated as confirmed diagnoses rather than uncertain diagnoses
