# ICD-10-CM Factors Influencing Health Status Codes
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Factors Influencing Health Status Codes

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes are provided to deal with encounters for circumstances other than a disease or injury.
- Factors Influencing Health Status and Contact with Health Services codes (Z00-Z99) are used when circumstances other than disease or injury are recorded as diagnosis or problems.
- These codes address occasions where non-disease circumstances are documented as the primary reason for the encounter.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The circumstance must be recorded as a diagnosis or problem on the claim.
- The circumstance must not be a disease or injury.
