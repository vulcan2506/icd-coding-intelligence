# Drug-Drug and Drug-Alcohol Interaction Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Drug-Drug and Drug-Alcohol Interaction Coding Standards

**Key Behaviors:**
- Classify reactions resulting from drug(s) and alcohol interaction as poisoning
- Refer to Section I.C.4 for coding poisoning results from insulin pump malfunctions
- Refer to Section I.B.10 for coding Sequela (Late Effects)
- Distinguish between interaction-based poisoning and device malfunction poisoning
- Apply specific section references based on the nature of the adverse event

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must confirm the reaction results from drug-alcohol interaction
- Section I.C.4 must be consulted for insulin pump malfunction cases
- Section I.B.10 must be consulted for late effect cases
