# Drug-Drug and Drug-Alcohol Interaction Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Drug-Drug and Drug-Alcohol Interaction Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Classifies reactions resulting from drug(s) and alcohol interaction as poisoning
- Directs coding for insulin pump malfunction poisoning to Section I.C.4
- Directs coding for late effects (sequela) to Section I.B.10
