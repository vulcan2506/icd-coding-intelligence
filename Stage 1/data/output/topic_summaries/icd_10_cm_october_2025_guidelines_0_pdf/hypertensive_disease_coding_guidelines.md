# Hypertensive Disease Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Hypertensive Disease Coding with Heart Conditions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign code from category I11 for hypertension with heart conditions classified to I50.-, I51.4, I51.89, or I51.9, using additional codes from I50 or I51 to identify the heart condition.
- Assign code from category I11 for hypertension with heart conditions classified to I51.5 or I51.7 without assigning an additional code for the specific heart condition.
- Code heart conditions (I50.-, I51.4-I51.9) and hypertension separately if the provider documents they are unrelated, sequencing based on admission circumstances.
- Assign appropriate codes from categories I10-I15 for diagnostic statements referring to existing hypertension under control by therapy.
- Assign appropriate codes from categories I10-I15 for uncontrolled hypertension, whether untreated or not responding to therapy.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Provider must document that heart conditions are unrelated to hypertension to justify separate coding.
- Provider must document uncertainty or specific conditions (e.g., myocardial degeneration) to determine if additional codes are required.
- Coding must follow sequence rules based on the circumstances of the admission or encounter when conditions are unrelated.
