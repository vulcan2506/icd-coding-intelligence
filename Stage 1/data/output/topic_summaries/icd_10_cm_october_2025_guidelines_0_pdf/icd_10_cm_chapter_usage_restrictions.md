# ICD-10-CM Chapter Usage Restrictions
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Chapter Usage Restrictions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in this chapter are never for use on the maternal record.
- Codes from Chapter 15 (obstetric) are never permitted on the newborn record.
- Chapter 16 codes may be used throughout the life of the patient if the condition is still present.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be present to use Chapter 16 codes.
- Maternal record must not contain codes from this chapter.
- Newborn record must not contain codes from Chapter 15.
