# Ambulatory Surgery Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Ambulatory Surgery Diagnosis Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code the diagnosis for which the surgery was performed in ambulatory settings.
- Select the postoperative diagnosis if it is known to be different from the preoperative diagnosis at the time of confirmation.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The diagnosis must be confirmed at the time of surgery.
- The postoperative diagnosis must be definitively known to differ from the preoperative diagnosis.
