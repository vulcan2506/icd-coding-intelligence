# Inpatient Z-Code Classification Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Inpatient Z-Code Principal Diagnosis Restrictions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Z codes may only be reported as the principal/first-listed diagnosis in specific scenarios.
- The exception applies when there are multiple encounters on the same day and medical records are combined.
- Z00.6 is excluded from the general Z00 principal diagnosis rule.
- Specific Z code mappings exist for infertility and pregnancy supervision scenarios.
- Administrative and special examinations without complaints are restricted to principal diagnosis status.
- Z01 codes (Encounter for other special examination) are restricted from being the principal diagnosis.
- Z02 codes (Encounter for administrative examination) are restricted from being the principal diagnosis.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Multiple encounters must occur on the same day.
- Medical records for the encounters must be combined.
- The condition must not fall under the Z00.6 exception category.
- The diagnosis must be the primary reason for the combined encounter.
