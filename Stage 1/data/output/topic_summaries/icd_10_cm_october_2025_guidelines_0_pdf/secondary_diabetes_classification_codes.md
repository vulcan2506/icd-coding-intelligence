# Secondary Diabetes Classification Codes
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary Diabetes Classification Logic

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes under categories E08, E09, and E13 identify complications or manifestations associated with secondary diabetes mellitus
- Secondary diabetes is strictly classified only when caused by another specific condition or event
- Valid underlying causes include cystic fibrosis, malignant neoplasm of pancreas, pancreatectomy, adverse drug effects, or poisoning
- These codes distinguish secondary diabetes from primary diabetes types not linked to external conditions
- Classification requires explicit documentation of the causative condition or event

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Underlying condition or event must be documented to justify E08, E09, or E13 assignment
- Causality between the secondary condition and diabetes must be established
