# Injury Coding and Sequencing Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Injury Coding and Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign separate codes for each injury unless a combination code is provided.
- Do not assign codes from category T07 (Unspecified multiple injuries) in the inpatient setting unless more specific information is unavailable.
- Do not use traumatic injury codes (S00-T14.9) for normal, healing surgical wounds or to identify complications of surgical wounds.
- Sequence the code for the most serious injury first, as determined by the provider and the focus of treatment.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Provider must determine the most serious injury based on the focus of treatment.
- Documentation must support the assignment of specific injury codes rather than unspecified multiple injury codes in inpatient settings.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using codes from category T07 for inpatient claims when specific injury information is available.
- Coding normal, healing surgical wounds or surgical wound complications using traumatic injury codes (S00-T14.9).
