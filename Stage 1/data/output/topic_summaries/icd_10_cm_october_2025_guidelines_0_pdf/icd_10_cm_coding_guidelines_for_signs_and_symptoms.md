# ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines for Signs and Symptoms
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Coding of Signs and Symptoms

**Key Behaviors:**
- Signs and symptoms associated routinely with a disease process should not be assigned as additional codes

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be routinely associated with the disease process to be excluded
