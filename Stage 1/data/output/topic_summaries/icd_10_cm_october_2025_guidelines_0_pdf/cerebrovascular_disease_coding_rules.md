# Cerebrovascular Disease Coding Rules
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Cerebrovascular Disease Coding: I69 with I60-I67

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes from category I69 may be assigned on a health care record with codes from I60-I67
- Assignment requires the patient to have a current cerebrovascular disease
- Assignment requires the patient to have deficits from an old cerebrovascular disease
