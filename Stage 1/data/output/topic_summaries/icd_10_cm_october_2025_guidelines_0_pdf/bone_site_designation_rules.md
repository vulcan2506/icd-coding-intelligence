# Bone Site Designation Rules
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Bone Site Designation Rules

**Key Behaviors:**
- For conditions affecting the upper or lower end of a bone (e.g., avascular necrosis, osteoporosis), the bone site must be designated.
- If the affected portion is at the joint, the site designation must be the bone, not the joint.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must affect the upper or lower end of the bone.
- Condition must be documented as affecting the bone rather than the joint.
