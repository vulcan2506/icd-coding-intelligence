# M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes under M80 identify the specific site of the fracture.
- A code from category M80 should be used for any patient with known osteoporosis who suffers a fracture.
- This applies even if the patient had a minor fall or trauma that would not usually break a normal, healthy bone.
- Category M80 is designated for patients with a current pathological fracture at the time of an encounter.
- M80 codes must be used instead of traumatic fracture codes for osteoporosis-related fractures.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Patient must have known osteoporosis.
- Patient must have a current pathological fracture at the time of the encounter.
- The fracture must occur due to trauma that would not typically break a normal, healthy bone.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using traumatic fracture codes for osteoporosis-related fractures.
- Excluding M80 codes when the fracture resulted from a minor fall in a patient with known osteoporosis.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit requirement to use M80 codes regardless of the severity of the precipitating trauma if osteoporosis is known.
