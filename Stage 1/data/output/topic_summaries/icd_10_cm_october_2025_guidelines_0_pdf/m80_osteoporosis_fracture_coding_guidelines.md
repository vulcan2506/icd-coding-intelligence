# M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes under M80 identify the site of the fracture for patients with a current pathological fracture.
- A code from category M80 should be used for any patient with known osteoporosis who suffers a fracture, even if the patient had a minor fall or trauma, if that fall or trauma would not usually break a normal, healthy bone.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Patient must have a current pathological fracture at the time of the encounter.
- Patient must have known osteoporosis.
- The fracture must occur from trauma that would not usually break a normal, healthy bone.
