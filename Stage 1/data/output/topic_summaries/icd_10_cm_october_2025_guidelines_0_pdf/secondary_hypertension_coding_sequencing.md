# Secondary Hypertension Coding Sequencing
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary Hypertension Coding Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Two ICD-10-CM codes are required for diagnosis: one for the underlying etiology and one from category I15 for hypertension
- The order of the two codes is determined by the reason for the patient's admission or encounter
- The underlying etiology code identifies the cause of the secondary hypertension
- The I15 category code specifically identifies the hypertension condition
- Coding logic adapts based on whether the encounter is for the etiology or the hypertension

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Both etiology and I15 codes must be present on the claim
- Sequencing must align with the primary reason for the encounter
