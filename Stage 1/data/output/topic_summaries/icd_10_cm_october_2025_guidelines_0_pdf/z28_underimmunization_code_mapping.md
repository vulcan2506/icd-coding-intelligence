# Z28 Underimmunization Code Mapping
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Z28 Underimmunization Code Mapping and Related Encounter Mappings

**Key Behaviors:**
- Map Z28 (Underimmunization status) to Z29 for encounters regarding other prophylactic measures.
- Map Z28 (Underimmunization status) to Z40 for encounters regarding prophylactic surgery.
- Map Z28 (Underimmunization status) to Z41 for encounters regarding procedures for purposes other than remedying health state.
- Map Z76.3 (Healthy person accompanying sick person) to Z76.4 for other boarder to healthcare facility.
- Map Z76.3 (Healthy person accompanying sick person) to Z91.1- for patient's noncompliance with medical treatment and regimen.
- Map Z28 (Underimmunization status) to Z53 for encounters regarding procedures not carried out.
- Map Z76.3 (Healthy person accompanying sick person) to Z76.5 for encounters regarding malingerers.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The encounter must be specifically for underimmunization status to utilize the Z28 code.
- The encounter must be for prophylactic surgery to utilize the Z40 code.
- The encounter must be for procedures not intended to remedy a health state to utilize the Z41 code.
- The patient must be a healthy person accompanying a sick person to utilize the Z76.3 code.
- The patient must be a boarder to a healthcare facility to utilize the Z76.4 code.
- The encounter must not be for Z28.3 (Underimmunization status) to utilize the Z28 code.
- The encounter must not be for Z41.9 (Encounter for procedure for purposes other than remedying health state, unspecified) to utilize the Z41 code.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit mapping of Z28.3 (Underimmunization status) to Z29.
- Explicit mapping of Z28.3 (Underimmunization status) to Z40.
- Explicit mapping of Z28.3 (Underimmunization status) to Z41.
- Explicit mapping of Z76.3 to Z76.4 for boarder status.
- Explicit mapping of Z76.3 to Z91.1- for noncompliance.
- Explicit mapping of Z28 to Z53 for procedures not carried out.
- Explicit mapping of Z76.3 to Z76.5 for malingerers.
