# Diagnostic Statement Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Diagnostic Statement Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code diagnoses included in the final diagnostic statement (e.g., discharge summary, face sheet).
- Do not report resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions that have no bearing on the current stay.
- Use history codes (Z80-Z87) as secondary codes only if the historical condition or family history impacts current care or influences treatment.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Diagnosis must be present in the final diagnostic statement to be ordinarily coded.
- Resolved conditions must have a bearing on the current stay to be reported.
- History codes require documented impact on current care or treatment influence.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Coding resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions that have no bearing on the current stay.
