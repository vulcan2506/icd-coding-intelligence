# ICD-10-CM Pediatric Encounter Codes
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Pediatric Encounter Coding Standards

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign Z76.1 for encounters involving health supervision and care of foundlings
- Assign Z00.1- for routine child health examinations
- Assign Z38 for liveborn infants categorized by place of birth and type of delivery

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must confirm the specific pediatric condition or encounter type before code assignment
- Place of birth and type of delivery must be documented to support Z38 assignment
