# ICD-10-CM P07 Category Implementation
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM P07 Category Implementation

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes from category P07 are used for a child or adult who was premature or had a low birth weight as a newborn and this is affecting the patient's current health status.
- These codes are assigned when the newborn condition influences the current health status of the patient.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The patient must have been premature or had a low birth weight as a newborn.
- The condition must be affecting the patient's current health status.
- The patient must be a child or adult (not a newborn at the time of coding).
