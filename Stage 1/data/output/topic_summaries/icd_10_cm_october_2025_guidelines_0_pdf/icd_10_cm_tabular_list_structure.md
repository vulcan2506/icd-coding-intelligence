# ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure

**Key Behaviors:**
- All categories must be exactly 3 characters long.
- Subcategories are either 4 or 5 characters long.
- Codes may be 3, 4, 5, 6, or 7 characters long.
- A code with an applicable 7th character is considered invalid without that 7th character.
- The list uses an indented format to represent levels of subdivision.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Categories must be 3 characters.
- Subcategories must be 4 or 5 characters.
- Final level codes must include applicable 7th characters if required.
- Codes without required 7th characters are invalid.
- Structure must follow the indented hierarchy of categories, subcategories, and codes.
