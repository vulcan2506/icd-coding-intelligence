# ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure

**Key Behaviors:**
- Categories are always 3 characters and may have no further subdivision.
- Subcategories are either 4 or 5 characters.
- Codes may be 3, 4, 5, 6, or 7 characters.
- A code with an applicable 7th character is considered invalid without it.
- The list uses an indented format for ease of reference.
- Characters for categories, subcategories, and codes may be either a letter or a number.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- All categories must be exactly 3 characters.
- Subcategories must be 4 or 5 characters.
- Final level codes must include applicable 7th characters if required.
- Codes without required 7th characters are invalid.
- Structure follows hierarchy: Category -> Subcategory -> Code.
