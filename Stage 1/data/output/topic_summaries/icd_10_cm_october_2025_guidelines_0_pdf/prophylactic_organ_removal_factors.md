# Prophylactic Organ Removal Factors
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Prophylactic Organ Removal Factors

**Key Behaviors:**
- Factors influencing health status and contact with health services must be coded when related to prophylactic organ removal.
- These factors are categorized under Section I.C. 21 of the ICD-10-CM guidelines.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The condition must be explicitly documented as a factor influencing health status.
- The condition must be related to prophylactic organ removal procedures.
