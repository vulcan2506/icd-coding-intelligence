# Prophylactic Organ Removal Factors
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Prophylactic Organ Removal Factors

**Key Behaviors:**
- Factors influencing health status and contact with health services must be coded under Section I.C. 21
- Prophylactic organ removal is categorized as a factor influencing health status rather than a disease process
- Documentation must explicitly identify the prophylactic nature of the organ removal to justify coding under this section

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Clinical documentation must specify the organ removed and the prophylactic intent
- The condition must be classified under Factors influencing health status and contact with health services (Section I.C. 21)
- Coding must reflect the preventive nature of the procedure rather than a treatment for an existing disease

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Coding prophylactic organ removal as a complication of an unrelated procedure
- Assigning codes for prophylactic removal under disease-specific categories without explicit documentation of the preventive intent

**New in this version:**
- Inclusion of specific external cause codes for prophylactic organ removal if applicable to the encounter
- Mandatory linkage of the removal event to the specific health status factor code in Section I.C. 21
