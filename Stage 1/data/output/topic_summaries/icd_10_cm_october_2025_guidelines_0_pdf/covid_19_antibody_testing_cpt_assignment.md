# COVID-19 Antibody Testing CPT Assignment
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** COVID-19 Antibody Testing CPT Assignment

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign Z01.84 for antibody testing not performed to confirm current infection nor as follow-up after resolution
- Follow applicable guidelines if testing is performed to confirm a current COVID-19 infection
- Refer to guideline I.C.1.g.1.j for follow-up testing after a COVID-19 infection

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Testing must not be performed to confirm a current COVID-19 infection
- Testing must not be a follow-up test after resolution of COVID-19
- Documentation must clarify the purpose of the encounter regarding current infection status
