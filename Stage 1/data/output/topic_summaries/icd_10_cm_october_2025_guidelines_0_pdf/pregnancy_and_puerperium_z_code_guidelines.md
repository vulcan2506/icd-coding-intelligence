# Pregnancy and Puerperium Z-Code Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Pregnancy and Puerperium Z-Code Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in category Z34 (Encounter for supervision of normal pregnancy) are always first listed and must not be used with any other code from the OB chapter.
- Category Z3A (Weeks of gestation) codes should not be assigned for pregnancies with abortive outcomes, elective termination, or postpartum conditions.
- The outcome of delivery (category Z37) should be included on all maternal delivery records as a secondary code.
- Z codes for family planning or procreative management should be included on obstetric records during pregnancy or postpartum if applicable.
- The date of admission must be used to determine weeks of gestation for inpatient admissions encompassing more than one gestational week.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Z34 codes must be the first listed code when no problems or complications from the Obstetrics chapter exist.
- Category Z37 codes must be secondary and cannot be used on newborn records.
- Z3A codes are invalid for abortive outcomes, elective terminations, or postpartum conditions.
- Clinical documentation must specify the date of admission to calculate gestational weeks for multi-week inpatient stays.
- Family planning or procreative management Z codes are mandatory on obstetric records if applicable to the encounter.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using Z34 codes in conjunction with other codes from the OB chapter.
- Assigning Z3A codes to pregnancies with abortive outcomes or elective terminations.
- Using Z37 codes on newborn records instead of maternal records.
- Failing to list Z34 codes as the primary code for routine prenatal visits.
- Omitting Z37 codes on maternal delivery records.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit inclusion of Z30 (Contraceptive management), Z31 (Procreative management), and Z32.2 (Childbirth instruction) on obstetric records.
- Mandatory use of Z37 for all maternal delivery records regardless of delivery method.
- Requirement to exclude Z3A codes for specific non-applicable conditions like postpartum care.
- Standardization of gestational week calculation based strictly on admission date for inpatient stays.
- Clarification that Z34 codes are reserved strictly for normal pregnancy supervision without complications.
