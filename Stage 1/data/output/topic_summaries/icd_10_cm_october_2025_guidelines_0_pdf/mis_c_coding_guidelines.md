# MIS-C Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** MIS-C Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign code U07.1 as principal diagnosis for individuals with MIS and current COVID-19
- Assign code M35.81 as additional diagnosis for Multisystem inflammatory syndrome
- Assign code U09.9 for individuals with history of COVID-19 who develop MIS
- Assign code Z20.822 for individuals with suspected exposure to COVID-19 who develop MIS without current infection or history
- Assign additional codes for any associated complications of MIS

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Current COVID-19 infection must be documented to assign U07.1 as principal diagnosis
- History of COVID-19 must be documented to assign U09.9
- Known or suspected exposure to COVID-19 without current infection or history must be documented to assign Z20.822
- MIS diagnosis must be documented to assign M35.81
- Associated complications must be documented to assign additional codes
