# Factors Influencing Health Status and Contact
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Factors Influencing Health Status and Contact

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code factors influencing health status and contact with health services when documented as the reason for encounter or service.
- Include follow-up care as a specific factor influencing health status and contact with health services.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be documented as a factor influencing health status or contact with health services.
- Documentation must specify the nature of the contact or the influence on health status.
