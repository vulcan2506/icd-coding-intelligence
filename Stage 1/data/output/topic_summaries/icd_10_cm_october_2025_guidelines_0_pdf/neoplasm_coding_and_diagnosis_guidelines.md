# Neoplasm Coding and Diagnosis Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Neoplasm Coding and Complication Treatment

**Key Behaviors:**
- Designate the complication as the principal/first-listed diagnosis when the encounter is for treatment of a complication resulting from a surgical procedure performed for the treatment of the neoplasm.
- Determine if the code for the neoplasm should also be assigned by consulting guidelines regarding the coding of a current malignancy versus personal history.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The encounter must be specifically for the treatment of a complication.
- The complication must result from a surgical procedure previously performed for the treatment of the neoplasm.
- The neoplasm status (current malignancy vs. personal history) must be verified before assigning the neoplasm code.
