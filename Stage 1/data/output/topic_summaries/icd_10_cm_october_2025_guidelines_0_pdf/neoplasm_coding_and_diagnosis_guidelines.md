# Neoplasm Coding and Diagnosis Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Neoplasm Coding and Complication Management

**Key Behaviors:**
- Designate the complication resulting from a surgical procedure for neoplasm treatment as the principal/first-listed diagnosis.
- Determine if a code for the neoplasm should also be assigned by referencing guidelines on current malignancy versus personal history.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The encounter must be for treatment of a complication resulting from a surgical procedure performed for the treatment of the neoplasm.
- Documentation must support the classification of the condition as a complication of care.
