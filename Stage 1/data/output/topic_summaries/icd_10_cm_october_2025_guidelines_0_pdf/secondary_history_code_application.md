# Secondary History Code Application
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary History Code Application

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code all documented conditions that coexist at the time of the encounter/visit and that require or affect patient care, treatment or management.
- Do not code conditions that were previously treated and no longer exist.
- History codes (categories Z80-Z87) may be used as secondary codes if the historical condition or family history has an impact on current care or influences treatment.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Conditions must be documented as coexisting at the time of the encounter.
- Conditions must require or affect patient care, treatment, or management.
- Historical conditions must have a demonstrable impact on current care or treatment decisions.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Coding previously treated conditions that no longer exist as primary diagnoses.
- Assigning history codes (Z80-Z87) as secondary codes when the historical condition does not influence current care.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit inclusion of family history codes (Z80-Z87) as secondary codes when they influence treatment.
