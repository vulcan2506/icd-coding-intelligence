# Postpartum and Neoplasm Follow-up Code Assignments
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Postpartum and Neoplasm Follow-up Code Assignments

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes Z08 and Z09 may be assigned following any type of completed treatment modality, including both medical and surgical treatments.
- Code Z39 is used for encounters for maternal postpartum care and examination.
- Follow-up codes are applicable after the completion of treatment for malignant neoplasms.
- Follow-up codes are applicable after the completion of treatment for conditions other than malignant neoplasms.
- Treatment modality type (medical or surgical) does not restrict the assignment of Z08 or Z09 codes.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The treatment for the neoplasm or condition must be completed before assigning Z08 or Z09.
- The encounter must be specifically for a follow-up examination.
- The condition must be a malignant neoplasm for Z08 or a condition other than malignant neoplasm for Z09.
- The patient must be undergoing postpartum care for Z39.
- Documentation must support that the treatment has been completed.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit confirmation that Z08 and Z09 apply to both medical and surgical treatment modalities.
- Inclusion of Z39 as a distinct code for maternal postpartum care and examination.
