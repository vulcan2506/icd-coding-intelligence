# I27 Pulmonary Hypertension Coding Guidelines
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** I27 Pulmonary Hypertension Classification and Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Classify pulmonary hypertension to category I27, Other pulmonary heart diseases.
- Code secondary pulmonary hypertension (I27.1, I27.2-) along with any associated conditions or adverse effects of drugs or toxins.
- Sequence codes based on the reason for the encounter, with specific exceptions for adverse effects of drugs.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must identify the condition as secondary pulmonary hypertension to assign I27.1 or I27.2-.
- Associated conditions or adverse effects of drugs/toxins must be documented to be coded alongside the primary hypertension code.
- Sequencing must prioritize the reason for the encounter unless the condition is an adverse effect of drugs.
