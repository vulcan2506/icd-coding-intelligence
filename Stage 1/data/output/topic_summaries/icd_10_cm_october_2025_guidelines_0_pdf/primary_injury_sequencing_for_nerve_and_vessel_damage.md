# Primary Injury Sequencing for Nerve and Vessel Damage
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Primary Injury Sequencing for Nerve and Vessel Damage

**Key Behaviors:**
- When a primary injury results in minor damage to peripheral nerves or blood vessels, the primary injury is sequenced first with additional code(s) for injuries to nerves and spinal cord (such as category S04), and/or injury to blood vessels (such as category S15).
- When the primary injury is to the blood vessels or nerves, that injury should be sequenced first.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must clearly identify the primary injury versus the resulting minor damage to nerves or vessels.
- Additional codes for nerve or vessel injuries must be assigned only when the damage is minor relative to the primary injury.
