# Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate

**Key Behaviors:**
- Mandates collection of present on admission information for inpatient admissions to general acute care hospitals or other facilities subject to law or regulation.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Claims must involve inpatient admissions to general acute care hospitals or other facilities subject to a law or regulation mandating collection of present on admission information.
