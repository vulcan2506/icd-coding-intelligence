# Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate

**Key Behaviors:**
- Mandates collection of present on admission information for all inpatient admissions to general acute care hospitals
- Applies to facilities subject to specific laws or regulations requiring this data collection
- Requires documentation to distinguish conditions present on admission from those arising during the stay

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Claims must involve inpatient admissions to general acute care hospitals
- Facilities must be subject to a law or regulation mandating the collection of present on admission information
- Accurate documentation of condition status at the time of admission is required for valid coding
