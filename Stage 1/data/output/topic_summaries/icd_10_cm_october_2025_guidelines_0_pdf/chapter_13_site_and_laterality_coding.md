# Chapter 13 Site and Laterality Coding
*Source: icd_10_cm_october_2025_guidelines_0.pdf*

**Feature:** Chapter 13 Site and Laterality Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes within Chapter 13 must include site and laterality designations
- The site designation identifies the specific bone, joint, or muscle involved

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Site designation must accurately reflect the anatomical bone, joint, or muscle
- Laterality must be explicitly designated for all applicable codes in this chapter
