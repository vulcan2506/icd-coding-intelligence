# Pregnancy and Puerperium Z-Code Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Pregnancy and Puerperium Z-Code Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in category Z34 are always first listed and must not be used with any other code from the OB chapter for routine prenatal or postpartum care.
- Category Z3A codes for weeks of gestation are assigned to provide additional pregnancy information but are excluded for abortive outcomes, elective terminations, and postpartum conditions.
- The outcome of delivery (category Z37) must be included on all maternal delivery records as a secondary code.
- Family planning or procreative management Z codes should be included on obstetric records during pregnancy or the postpartum stage if applicable.
- Category Z3A codes are not applicable to postpartum conditions.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Z34 codes must be the first listed code when no obstetrical problems or complications exist.
- Z37 outcome codes must be present on all maternal delivery records.
- Z37 codes must not be assigned to newborn records.
- Z3A codes must be based on the date of admission for inpatient admissions spanning multiple gestational weeks.
- Z3A codes must not be assigned for pregnancies with abortive outcomes or elective terminations.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using Z34 codes in conjunction with other codes from the OB chapter for routine care.
- Assigning Z3A codes for postpartum conditions.
- Assigning Z3A codes for pregnancies with abortive outcomes.
- Assigning Z3A codes for elective termination of pregnancy.
- Using Z37 outcome codes on newborn records.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit instruction to use the date of admission to determine weeks of gestation for multi-week inpatient admissions.
- Mandatory inclusion of family planning or procreative management Z codes on obstetric records when applicable.
- Clarification that Z34 codes are exclusively for circumstances where no OB chapter problems exist.
