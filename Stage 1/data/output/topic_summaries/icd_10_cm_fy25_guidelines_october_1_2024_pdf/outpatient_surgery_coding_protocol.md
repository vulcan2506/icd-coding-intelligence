# Outpatient Surgery Coding Protocol
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Outpatient Surgery Coding Protocol

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code the reason for the surgery as the first-listed diagnosis for outpatient/same-day surgery encounters, even if the surgery is not performed due to a contraindication.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The encounter must be an outpatient or same-day surgery setting.
- The reason for the surgery must be documented as the primary reason for the encounter.
