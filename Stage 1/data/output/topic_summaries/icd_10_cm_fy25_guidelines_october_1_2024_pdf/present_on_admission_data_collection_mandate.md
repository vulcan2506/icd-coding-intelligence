# Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Present on Admission Data Collection Mandate

**Key Behaviors:**
- Mandates collection of present on admission information for inpatient admissions to general acute care hospitals
- Applies to facilities subject to specific laws or regulations requiring POA data

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Inpatient admission status
- Facility must be subject to a law or regulation mandating POA collection
