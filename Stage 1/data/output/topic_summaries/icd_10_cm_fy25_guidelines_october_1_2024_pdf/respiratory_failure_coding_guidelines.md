# Respiratory Failure Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Respiratory Failure Principal Diagnosis Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign codes from subcategory J96.0 or J96.2 as principal diagnosis when the condition is chiefly responsible for hospital admission.
- Ensure selection is supported by the Alphabetic Index and Tabular List.
- Yield to chapter-specific coding guidelines (e.g., obstetrics, poisoning, HIV, newborn) that provide sequencing direction.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Condition must be established after study to be chiefly responsible for occasioning the admission.
- Selection must be supported by the Alphabetic Index and Tabular List.
- Chapter-specific guidelines must take precedence over general respiratory failure guidelines.
