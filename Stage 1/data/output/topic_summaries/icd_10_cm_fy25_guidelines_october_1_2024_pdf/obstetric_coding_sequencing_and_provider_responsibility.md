# Obstetric Coding Sequencing and Provider Responsibility
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Obstetric Coding Sequencing and Provider Responsibility

**Key Behaviors:**
- Chapter 15 codes (O00-O9A) have sequencing priority over codes from other chapters.
- Additional codes from other chapters may be used to further specify conditions alongside Chapter 15 codes.
- If pregnancy is incidental to the encounter, code Z33.1 replaces any Chapter 15 codes.
- Providers must explicitly document that the condition being treated is not affecting the pregnancy.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Clinical documentation must explicitly state that the condition being treated is not affecting the pregnancy.
- Encounter must be confirmed as incidental to pregnancy to utilize code Z33.1 instead of Chapter 15 codes.
