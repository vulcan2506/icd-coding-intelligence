# Abuse and Neglect Coding Protocols
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Abuse and Neglect Coding Protocols

**Key Behaviors:**
- Sequence confirmed abuse/neglect codes (T74.-) followed by accompanying mental health or injury codes.
- Sequence suspected abuse/neglect codes (T76.-) followed by accompanying mental health or injury codes.
- Add external cause codes (X92-Y09) and perpetrator codes (Y07) only for confirmed abuse/neglect cases.
- Do not report external cause or perpetrator codes for suspected abuse/neglect cases.
- Use specific ruled-out encounter codes (Z04.71, Z04.72, Z04.41, Z04.42, Z04.81, Z04.82) instead of T76 codes when allegations are ruled out.
- Sequence the appropriate code from categories T74 or T76 followed by any accompanying mental health or injury code(s).
- Use specific Z04.71, Z04.72, Z04.41, Z04.42, Z04.81, or Z04.82 codes for ruled out cases of physical abuse, rape, sexual abuse, forced sexual exploitation, or forced labor exploitation.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must explicitly state abuse or neglect to assign confirmed codes (T74.-).
- Documentation must explicitly state suspected abuse or neglect to assign suspected codes (T76.-).
- Perpetrator code (Y07) requires the perpetrator to be known.
- External cause codes (X92-Y09) are required to identify the cause of physical injuries in confirmed cases.
- Ruled-out allegations require specific Z04 encounter codes rather than T76 codes.
- Perpetrator code (Y07) requires the perpetrator of the abuse to be known.
