# Abuse and Neglect Coding Protocols
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Abuse and Neglect Coding Protocols

**Key Behaviors:**
- Sequence confirmed abuse/neglect codes (T74.-) followed by mental health or injury codes, then external cause codes (X92-Y09) and perpetrator codes (Y07) if known.
- Code suspected abuse/neglect as T76.- only if not ruled out; if ruled out, use specific Z04.71, Z04.72, Z04.41, Z04.42, Z04.81, or Z04.82 codes instead.
- Do not report external cause or perpetrator codes for suspected cases of abuse or neglect.
- Assign T74.- for confirmed abuse/neglect documented in the medical record.
- Apply specific Z04 encounter codes for ruled-out allegations of physical abuse, rape, sexual abuse, forced sexual exploitation, or forced labor exploitation.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must explicitly state 'abuse' or 'neglect' to assign confirmed codes (T74.-).
- Documentation must explicitly state 'suspected' to assign suspected codes (T76.-).
- External cause codes (X92-Y09) and perpetrator codes (Y07) are mandatory for confirmed cases to identify injury causes and perpetrators.
- If a suspected case is ruled out during an encounter, a specific Z04.- code must be used rather than a T76.- code.
- Perpetrator codes (Y07) are required only when the perpetrator of the abuse is known.
- Documentation must explicitly reference 'abuse in a pregnant patient' to trigger specific coding considerations per Section I.C.15.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using T76.- codes for cases of suspected abuse or neglect that have been ruled out during the encounter.
- Reporting external cause or perpetrator codes for suspected cases of abuse or neglect.
- Failing to sequence confirmed abuse/neglect codes before accompanying mental health or injury codes.
- Using T76.- codes for ruled-out allegations of rape, sexual abuse, forced sexual exploitation, or forced labor exploitation instead of Z04.- codes.
- Omitting external cause codes for confirmed abuse cases where physical injuries are present.

**New in this version:**
- Mandatory use of Z04.71 and Z04.72 for ruled-out physical adult and child abuse encounters.
- Mandatory use of Z04.41 and Z04.42 for ruled-out adult and child rape encounters.
- Mandatory use of Z04.81 and Z04.82 for ruled-out forced sexual exploitation and forced labor exploitation encounters.
- Requirement to add perpetrator code (Y07) specifically when the perpetrator is known in confirmed cases.
- Explicit instruction to sequence T74.- codes before any accompanying mental health or injury codes.
