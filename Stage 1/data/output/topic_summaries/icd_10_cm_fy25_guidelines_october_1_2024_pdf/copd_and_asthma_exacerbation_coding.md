# COPD and Asthma Exacerbation Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** COPD and Asthma Exacerbation Coding Standards

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in categories J44 and J45 distinguish between uncomplicated cases and those in acute exacerbation
- An acute exacerbation is defined as a worsening or decompensation of a chronic condition
- An acute exacerbation is not equivalent to an infection superimposed on a chronic condition
- An exacerbation may be triggered by an infection but is distinct from the infection itself
- Documentation must confirm the worsening or decompensation of the chronic condition to assign exacerbation codes

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must explicitly describe the worsening or decompensation of the chronic condition
- Clinicians must distinguish between a superimposed infection and an exacerbation of the underlying chronic condition
