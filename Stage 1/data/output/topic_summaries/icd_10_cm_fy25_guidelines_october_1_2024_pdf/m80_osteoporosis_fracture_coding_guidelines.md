# M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** M80 Osteoporosis Fracture Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes under M80 identify the specific site of the fracture.
- A code from category M80 should be used for any patient with known osteoporosis who suffers a fracture, even if the patient had a minor fall or trauma.
- This applies if the minor fall or trauma would not usually break a normal, healthy bone.
- Category M80 is designated for patients with a current pathological fracture at the time of an encounter.
- M80 codes must be used instead of traumatic fracture codes for osteoporosis-related fractures.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Patient must have known osteoporosis.
- Patient must have a current pathological fracture at the time of the encounter.
- The fracture must be caused by trauma that would not typically break a normal, healthy bone.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using traumatic fracture codes for osteoporosis-related fractures when M80 is applicable.
- Excluding M80 codes when the trauma was minor and insufficient to break healthy bone.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit requirement to use M80 codes even in the presence of minor trauma if the patient has known osteoporosis.
