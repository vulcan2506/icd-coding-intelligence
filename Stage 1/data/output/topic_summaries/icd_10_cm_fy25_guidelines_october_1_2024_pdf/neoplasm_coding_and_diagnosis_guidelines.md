# Neoplasm Coding and Diagnosis Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Neoplasm Coding and Complication Management

**Key Behaviors:**
- Designate the complication as the principal/first-listed diagnosis when an encounter is for treatment of a complication resulting from a surgical procedure performed for the treatment of the neoplasm.
- Determine if the code for the neoplasm should also be assigned by consulting guidelines regarding the coding of a current malignancy versus personal history.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The encounter must be specifically for the treatment of a complication.
- The complication must result from a surgical procedure performed for the treatment of the neoplasm.
- The provider must document the complication as the reason for the encounter.
