# Z52 Donor Codes Implementation
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Z52 Donor Codes Implementation

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in category Z52 are used for living individuals who are donating blood or other body tissue.
- These codes apply to individuals donating for others as well as for self-donations.
- Codes are not used to identify cadaveric donations.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The donor must be a living individual.
- The donation must involve blood or other body tissue.
