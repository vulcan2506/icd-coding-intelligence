# COVID-19 Sequencing Guidelines for Pregnancy
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** COVID-19 Sequencing Guidelines for Pregnancy

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code O98.5- is sequenced as the principal/first-listed diagnosis when COVID-19 is the reason for admission/encounter during pregnancy, childbirth, or the puerperium.
- Code U07.1 and codes for associated manifestations are assigned as additional diagnoses when COVID-19 is the reason for admission/encounter.
- The appropriate code for the reason for admission/encounter is sequenced as the principal/first-listed diagnosis if the reason is unrelated to COVID-19 but the patient tests positive during the encounter.
- Codes O98.5- and U07.1 are assigned as additional diagnoses when the admission reason is unrelated to COVID-19 but the patient tests positive.
- Codes from Chapter 15 always take sequencing priority over other codes.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- COVID-19 must be the reason for admission/encounter to sequence O98.5- as principal.
- Documentation must indicate the patient tests positive for COVID-19 during the encounter for U07.1 assignment.
- Associated manifestation codes must be assigned alongside U07.1.
- Chapter 15 codes must be prioritized in sequencing regardless of other conditions.
- O98.5- and U07.1 must be assigned as additional diagnoses when the primary reason for admission is unrelated to COVID-19.
