# ICD-10-CM Coding for Radiation and Chemotherapy Admissions
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Coding for Radiation and Chemotherapy Admissions

**Key Behaviors:**
- For external beam radiotherapy, immunotherapy, or chemotherapy admissions, Z51.0, Z51.11, or Z51.12 is the principal diagnosis
- Complication codes (e.g., uncontrolled nausea, vomiting, dehydration) are sequenced after the principal therapy code
- For radioactive element insertion or implantation (brachytherapy) admissions, the malignancy code is the principal diagnosis
- Complication codes are sequenced after the malignancy code for brachytherapy admissions
- The principal diagnosis selection depends on whether the admission is for therapy or for implantation
