# ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines for Signs and Symptoms
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines for Signs and Symptoms

**Key Behaviors:**
- Signs and symptoms associated routinely with a disease process should not be assigned as additional codes unless otherwise instructed by the classification.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Clinical documentation must explicitly indicate that a sign or symptom is not routinely associated with the disease process to justify additional coding.
