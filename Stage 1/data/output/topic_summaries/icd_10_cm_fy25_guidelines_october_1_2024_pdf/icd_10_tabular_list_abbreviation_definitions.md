# ICD-10 Tabular List Abbreviation Definitions
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Tabular List Abbreviation Definitions

**Key Behaviors:**
- NEC abbreviation represents 'other specified' conditions
- NEC entries identify codes used when a specific code is not available for a condition
- NOS abbreviation is equivalent to unspecified
- Abbreviations are defined within the ICD-10 Tabular List structure
- Abbreviations guide coding decisions for conditions lacking specific codes
