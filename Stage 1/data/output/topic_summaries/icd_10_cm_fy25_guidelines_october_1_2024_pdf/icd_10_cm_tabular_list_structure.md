# ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Tabular List Structure

**Key Behaviors:**
- Categories are strictly 3 characters and may have no further subdivision.
- Subcategories are either 4 or 5 characters.
- Final codes may be 3, 4, 5, 6, or 7 characters.
- Codes with applicable 7th characters are still referred to as codes, not subcategories.
- A code with an applicable 7th character is considered invalid without it.
- Characters for categories, subcategories, and codes may be either a letter or a number.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- All categories must be exactly 3 characters.
- Subcategories must be exactly 4 or 5 characters.
- Final codes must include applicable 7th characters if they exist for that code.
- A code with an applicable 7th character is invalid if the 7th character is missing.
- The list uses an indented format for reference.
