# UHDDS Other Diagnoses Reporting Definition
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** UHDDS Other Diagnoses Reporting Definition

**Key Behaviors:**
- Report additional clinically significant conditions affecting patient care requiring evaluation, treatment, procedures, extended stay, or increased nursing care.
- Include all conditions coexisting at admission, developing subsequently, or affecting treatment/length of stay.
- Exclude diagnoses relating to earlier episodes with no bearing on the current hospital stay.
- Apply definitions to inpatients in acute care, short-term, long-term care, psychiatric, home health, rehab, nursing homes, and hospice settings.
- Provider is responsible for listing diagnoses in the patient record when ICD-10-CM Index/Tabular List provide no direction.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must require clinical evaluation, therapeutic treatment, diagnostic procedures, extended length of stay, or increased nursing care/monitoring.
- Condition must coexist at time of admission, develop subsequently, or affect treatment received and/or length of stay.
- Condition must have a bearing on the current hospital stay (exclude earlier episode diagnoses with no bearing).
- Application applies to all non-outpatient settings including acute care, short-term, long-term care, psychiatric hospitals, home health, rehab, nursing homes, and hospice.
- Use ICD-10-CM Alphabetic Index and Tabular List for direction; if none provided, rely on provider's listing of diagnoses.

**Superseded Guidance:**
- The July 31, 1985 Federal Register (Vol. 50, No. 147), pp. 31038-40 is cited as the original source for UHDDS data elements and definitions.
