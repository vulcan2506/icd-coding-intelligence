# Perinatal Newborn Code Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Perinatal Newborn Code Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Refer to Section I.C.16 for further instruction on the use of newborn codes.
- Assign Z76.1 for encounters regarding health supervision and care of foundlings.
- Assign Z00.1- for encounters for routine child health examinations.
- Assign Z38 for liveborn infants based on place of birth and type of delivery.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must specify the place of birth and type of delivery to assign Z38 codes correctly.
- Encounter purpose must be verified as health supervision for foundlings to use Z76.1.
- Encounter must be for routine child health examination to justify Z00.1- assignment.
