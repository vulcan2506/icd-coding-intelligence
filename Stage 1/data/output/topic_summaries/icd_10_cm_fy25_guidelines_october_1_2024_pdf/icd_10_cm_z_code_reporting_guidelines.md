# ICD-10-CM Z Code Reporting Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Z Code Reporting Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Z codes may be assigned as appropriate to further explain reasons for healthcare services, including transfers between facilities.
- Assign as many codes as necessary to fully explain each healthcare encounter.
- Use any available documentation to assign appropriate external cause of morbidity and Z codes when patient history is limited.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Codes must be assigned based on whether they are designated as principal/first-listed diagnosis only, secondary diagnosis only, or both depending on circumstances.
- Documentation must support the assignment of Z codes to provide additional information relevant to the patient encounter.

**New in this version:**
- Specific Z codes listed include Z59.0- (Homelessness), Z59.1 (Inadequate housing), Z59.5 (Extreme poverty), Z75.1-Z75.4 (Facility/Agency unavailability), Z76.2 (Health supervision), and Z99.12 (Respirator dependence during power failure).
- External cause of morbidity codes and the listed Z codes are not an all-inclusive list; other applicable codes may be used based on documentation.
