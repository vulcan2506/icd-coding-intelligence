# FY 2025 ICD-10-CM Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** FY 2025 ICD-10-CM Neoplasm Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Primary malignant neoplasms overlapping two or more contiguous sites must be classified to the subcategory/code .8 ('overlapping lesion') unless specifically indexed elsewhere.
- Malignant neoplasms of ectopic tissue must be coded to the site of origin mentioned (e.g., ectopic pancreatic malignant neoplasm coded to pancreas).
- The neoplasm table in the Alphabetic Index should be referenced first, but if the histological term is documented, that term must be referenced first to determine the appropriate column in the Neoplasm Table.
- The Tabular List must be referenced to verify the correct code and ensure a more specific site code does not exist.
- Codes for genetic susceptibility to cancer (Z15.0) are referenced under Section I.C.21 regarding factors influencing health status.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- It is necessary to determine from the record if the neoplasm is benign, in-situ, malignant, or of uncertain histologic behavior.
- If malignant, any secondary (metastatic) sites must also be determined.
- For multiple neoplasms of the same site that are not contiguous, codes for each site should be assigned.
- Adherence to these guidelines when assigning ICD-10-CM diagnosis codes is required under HIPAA.
- The instructions and conventions of the classification take precedence over guidelines.
- A joint effort between the healthcare provider and the coder is essential to achieve complete and accurate documentation, code assignment, and reporting of diagnoses and procedures.
- The importance of consistent, complete documentation in the medical record cannot be overemphasized; without such documentation accurate coding cannot be achieved.

**New in this version:**
- Updated sequencing rules for external causes of morbidity codes (Section I.B.2 and I.B.3).
- Revised chapter-specific coding guidelines structure (Section I.C).
- Clarified instructions for coding neoplasms overlapping site boundaries to subcategory .8.
- Specific guidance on coding malignant neoplasms of ectopic tissue to the site of origin.
- Updated reference hierarchy for neoplasm coding: Histological term > Neoplasm Table > Tabular List verification.
