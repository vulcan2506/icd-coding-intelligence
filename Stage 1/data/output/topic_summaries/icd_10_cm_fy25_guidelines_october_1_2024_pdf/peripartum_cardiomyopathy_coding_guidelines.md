# Peripartum Cardiomyopathy Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Peripartum Cardiomyopathy Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code O90.3 is used for cardiomyopathy diagnosed in the third trimester or progressing months after delivery
- Condition must develop as a result of pregnancy
- Patient must not have pre-existing heart disease

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Diagnosis must occur in the third trimester of pregnancy or continue to progress months after delivery
- Cardiomyopathy must develop as a result of pregnancy
- Patient must not have pre-existing heart disease
