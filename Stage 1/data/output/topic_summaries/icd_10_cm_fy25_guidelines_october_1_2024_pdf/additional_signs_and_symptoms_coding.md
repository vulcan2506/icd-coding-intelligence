# Additional Signs and Symptoms Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Additional Signs and Symptoms Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code additional signs and symptoms that are not routinely associated with the primary disease process.
- Ensure the presence of these signs and symptoms is documented in the clinical record.
- Assign codes only when the signs and symptoms are present and distinct from the main diagnosis.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Clinical documentation must explicitly state the presence of the additional sign or symptom.
- The sign or symptom must not be an expected or integral part of the primary disease process.
