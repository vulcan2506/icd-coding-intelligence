# Pregnancy Substance Use Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Pregnancy Substance Use Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign O99.31 codes for alcohol use during pregnancy or postpartum
- Assign O99.33 codes for tobacco use during pregnancy or postpartum
- Assign O99.32 codes for drug use (illegal or prescription abuse) during pregnancy or postpartum
- Sequence abuse codes (O9A.3, O9A.4, O9A.5) first for suspected or confirmed abuse cases
- Assign secondary codes from F10, F17, F11-F16, F18-F19 categories to identify manifestations

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Patient must use alcohol, tobacco, or drugs during pregnancy or postpartum to assign O99.31, O99.33, or O99.32
- Secondary codes from F categories must be assigned to identify manifestations of substance use
- Abuse codes must be sequenced first if abuse is suspected or confirmed before assigning injury or perpetrator codes
- Documentation must indicate the type of tobacco product for O99.33 assignment
- Documentation must specify illegal drugs or inappropriate use/abuse of prescription drugs for O99.32 assignment
- Documentation must specify the type of tobacco product used to support O99.33 assignment.
- Documentation must specify illegal drugs or inappropriate use/abuse of prescription drugs to support O99.32 assignment.
