# Factors Influencing Health Status and Contact
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Factors Influencing Health Status and Contact

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code factors influencing health status and contact with health services when documented as such.
- Follow-up care is categorized under factors influencing health status and contact with health services.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be explicitly documented as a factor influencing health status or contact with health services.
- Documentation must specify the nature of the contact or influence on health status.
