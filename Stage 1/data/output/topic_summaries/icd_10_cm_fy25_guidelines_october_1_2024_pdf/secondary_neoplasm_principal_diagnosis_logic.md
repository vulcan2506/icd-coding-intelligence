# Secondary Neoplasm Principal Diagnosis Logic
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary Neoplasm Principal Diagnosis Logic

**Key Behaviors:**
- Secondary neoplasm is designated as the principal diagnosis when treatment is directed toward the secondary site only.
- This designation applies even if the primary malignancy is still present.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Patient must be admitted because of a primary neoplasm with metastasis.
- Treatment must be directed toward the secondary site only.
