# CKD Coding Sequencing Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** CKD Coding Sequencing and Contributing Conditions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Sequencing of CKD codes relative to other contributing conditions is determined by conventions in the Tabular List.
- Hypertensive chronic kidney disease requires specific reference to I.C.9 guidelines.
- Chronic kidney disease and kidney transplant complications require specific reference to I.C.19 guidelines.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must identify CKD alongside other serious conditions such as diabetes mellitus and hypertension.
- Coding must adhere to the specific conventions outlined in the ICD-10-CM Tabular List for sequencing.
