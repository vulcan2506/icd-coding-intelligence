# HIV Inpatient Guideline Exception
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** HIV Inpatient Guideline Exception

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code only confirmed cases of HIV infection/illness as an exception to the hospital inpatient guideline Section II, H.
- Confirmation does not require documentation of positive serology or culture for HIV.
- The provider's diagnostic statement that the patient is HIV positive or has an HIV-related illness is sufficient for coding.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Provider must issue a diagnostic statement confirming the patient is HIV positive or has an HIV-related illness.
- Documentation must explicitly confirm the case of HIV infection or illness.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Requiring documentation of positive serology or culture for HIV to confirm the case.

**New in this version:**
- Exception to hospital inpatient guideline Section II, H allowing coding based on provider diagnostic statement alone.
