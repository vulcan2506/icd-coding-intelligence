# I27 Pulmonary Hypertension Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** I27 Pulmonary Hypertension Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Classify pulmonary hypertension to category I27, Other pulmonary heart diseases.
- Code secondary pulmonary hypertension (I27.1, I27.2-) along with any associated conditions or adverse effects of drugs or toxins.
- Determine sequencing based on the reason for the encounter, with exceptions for adverse effects of drugs.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must identify the condition as pulmonary hypertension to assign category I27.
- Secondary pulmonary hypertension must be explicitly documented to use specific subcategories I27.1 or I27.2-.
- Associated conditions or adverse effects of drugs/toxins must be documented to be coded alongside the primary hypertension code.
