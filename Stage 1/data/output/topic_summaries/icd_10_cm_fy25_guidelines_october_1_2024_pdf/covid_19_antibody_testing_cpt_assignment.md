# COVID-19 Antibody Testing CPT Assignment
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** COVID-19 Antibody Testing CPT Assignment

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign Z01.84 for antibody testing not performed to confirm current infection or as a follow-up after resolution
- Follow applicable guidelines if testing is performed to confirm a current COVID-19 infection
- Refer to guideline I.C.1.g.1.j for follow-up testing after a COVID-19 infection

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The encounter must not be for confirming a current COVID-19 infection
- The encounter must not be a follow-up test after resolution of COVID-19

**New in this version:**
- Z01.84 is the specific code for encounters for antibody response examination under these conditions
