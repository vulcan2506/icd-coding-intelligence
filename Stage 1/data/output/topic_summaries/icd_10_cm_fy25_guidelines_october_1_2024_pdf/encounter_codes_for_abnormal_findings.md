# Encounter Codes for Abnormal Findings
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Encounter Codes for Abnormal Findings

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign the code for general medical examination with abnormal findings as the first-listed diagnosis if an abnormal finding is identified.
- An examination with abnormal findings refers to a condition newly identified or a change in severity of a chronic condition during a routine physical examination.
- A secondary code for the abnormal finding must also be coded alongside the encounter code.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- The abnormal finding must be newly identified or represent a change in severity of a chronic condition.
- The encounter must be a general medical examination or routine child health examination.
- The abnormal finding must be documented in the clinical record to support the assignment of the 'with abnormal findings' code.
