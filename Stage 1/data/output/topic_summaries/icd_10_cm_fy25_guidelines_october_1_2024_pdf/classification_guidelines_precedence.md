# Classification Guidelines Precedence
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Classification Guidelines Precedence

**Key Behaviors:**
- Conventions and general guidelines apply to all healthcare settings unless otherwise indicated.
- Chapter-specific guidelines apply to all healthcare settings unless otherwise indicated.
- Classification conventions and instructions take precedence over guidelines.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Conventions and instructions must be followed over conflicting guidelines.
- Guidelines are applicable unless explicitly indicated otherwise.
