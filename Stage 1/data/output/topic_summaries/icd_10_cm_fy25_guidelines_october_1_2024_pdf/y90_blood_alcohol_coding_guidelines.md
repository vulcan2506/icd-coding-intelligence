# Y90 Blood Alcohol Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Y90 Blood Alcohol Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign Y90 codes when blood alcohol level is documented and the provider documents a condition classifiable to category F10.
- The blood alcohol level does not need to be documented by the patient's provider to be coded.
- Refer to Section I.B.14 for documentation by clinicians other than the patient's provider.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Information regarding blood alcohol level must be documented.
- The patient's provider must document a condition classifiable to category F10 (Alcohol related disorders).
