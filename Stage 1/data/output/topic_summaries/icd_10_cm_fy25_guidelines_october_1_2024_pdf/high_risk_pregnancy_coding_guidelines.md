# High-Risk Pregnancy Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** High-Risk Pregnancy Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes from category O09 are intended for use only during the prenatal period.
- For complications during labor or delivery resulting from a high-risk pregnancy, assign applicable complication codes from Chapter 15.
- If there are no complications during labor or delivery, assign code O80, Encounter for full-term uncomplicated delivery.
- For routine prenatal outpatient visits for high-risk pregnancies, use a code from category O09 as the first-listed diagnosis.
- Secondary Chapter 15 codes may be used in conjunction with O09 codes if appropriate.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Category O09 codes must be assigned only during the prenatal period.
- Complication codes from Chapter 15 are mandatory for labor or delivery episodes with complications arising from high-risk pregnancy.
- Code O80 is mandatory for full-term uncomplicated delivery episodes without complications.
- Category O09 must be the first-listed diagnosis for routine prenatal outpatient visits involving high-risk pregnancies.
- Secondary Chapter 15 codes are permitted only when clinically appropriate alongside O09 codes.
