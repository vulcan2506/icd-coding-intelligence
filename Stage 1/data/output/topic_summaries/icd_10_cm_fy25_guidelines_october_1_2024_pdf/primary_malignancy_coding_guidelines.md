# Primary Malignancy Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Primary Malignancy Coding and Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Use primary malignancy code until treatment is completed if excised but further treatment is directed to that site.
- Use category Z85 code if malignancy is excised/eradicated with no further treatment and no evidence of existing malignancy.
- Assign Z85.0-Z85.85 codes only for the former site of a primary malignancy, not for secondary malignancy sites.
- Assign Z85.89 for the former site(s) of either a primary or secondary malignancy.
- Refer to Section I.C.21 for factors influencing health status and contact with health services regarding history.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Evidence of no further treatment directed to the site.
- No evidence of any existing primary malignancy at the site.
- Distinction between former site of primary vs. site of secondary malignancy for Z85 subcategories.
- Confirmation that malignancy has been excised or eradicated.
- Verification that treatment (surgery, radiation, chemotherapy) is completed or not directed to the site.
