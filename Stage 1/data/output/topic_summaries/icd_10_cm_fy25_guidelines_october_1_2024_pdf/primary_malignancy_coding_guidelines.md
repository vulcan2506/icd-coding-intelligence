# Primary Malignancy Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Primary Malignancy Coding and Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- Use primary malignancy code until treatment is completed if excised but further therapy is directed to that site
- Use category Z85 code if malignancy is excised/eradicated with no further treatment and no evidence of existing malignancy
- Assign Z85.0-Z85.85 codes only for the former site of a primary malignancy, not for secondary malignancy sites
- Assign Z85.89 for the former site(s) of either a primary or secondary malignancy
- Reference Section I.C.21 for factors influencing health status and contact with health services

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must confirm whether further treatment is directed to the site of the excised malignancy
- Documentation must confirm there is no evidence of existing primary malignancy at the site
- Documentation must distinguish between former sites of primary malignancy versus sites of secondary malignancy
- Clinical records must support the status of the malignancy (active, excised, or eradicated)
- Coding must align with Section I.C.21 guidelines regarding health status factors

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Using Z85.0-Z85.85 codes for the site of a secondary malignancy
- Assigning primary malignancy codes after the malignancy is eradicated with no further treatment
- Failing to query documentation regarding the status of excised malignancies
- Misinterpreting Z85.89 as exclusive to primary malignancy history
- Ignoring Section I.C.21 factors when determining health status codes

**New in this version:**
- Explicit distinction between Z85.0-Z85.85 and Z85.89 based on malignancy type (primary vs secondary)
- Mandatory use of Z85 codes when malignancy is eradicated with no further treatment directed to the site
- Requirement to reference Section I.C.21 for health status and contact with health services documentation
- Clarification that Z85 codes indicate the former site of malignancy, not current active sites
- Updated sequencing rules for complications associated with malignancies or their therapy
