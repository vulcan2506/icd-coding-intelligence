# Primary Injury Sequencing for Nerve and Vessel Damage
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Primary Injury Sequencing for Nerve and Vessel Damage

**Key Behaviors:**
- When a primary injury results in minor damage to peripheral nerves or blood vessels, the primary injury is sequenced first with additional codes for nerve or vessel injuries.
- When the primary injury is specifically to the blood vessels or nerves, that injury should be sequenced first.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Determine if the nerve or vessel damage is minor relative to the primary injury.
- Verify if the primary injury is specifically to the blood vessels or nerves.
