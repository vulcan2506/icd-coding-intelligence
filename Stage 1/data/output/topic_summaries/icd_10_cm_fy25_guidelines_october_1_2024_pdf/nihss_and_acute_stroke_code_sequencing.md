# NIHSS and Acute Stroke Code Sequencing
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** NIHSS and Acute Stroke Code Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- NIHSS codes (R29.7-) can be used in conjunction with acute stroke codes (I60-I63) to identify neurological status and stroke severity.
- Stroke scale codes must be sequenced after the acute stroke diagnosis code(s).
- At a minimum, the initial score documented must be reported.
- Facilities may choose to capture multiple stroke scale scores if desired.
- Section I.B.14. governs NIHSS stroke scale documentation by clinicians other than the patient's provider.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Acute stroke diagnosis codes (I60-I63) must be present for NIHSS codes to be sequenced after them.
- The initial NIHSS score documented in the medical record must be reported.
- Documentation must comply with Section I.B.14. for non-provider clinicians.
