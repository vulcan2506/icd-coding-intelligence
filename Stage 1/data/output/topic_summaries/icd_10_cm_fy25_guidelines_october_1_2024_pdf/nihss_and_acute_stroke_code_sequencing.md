# NIHSS and Acute Stroke Code Sequencing
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** NIHSS and Acute Stroke Code Sequencing

**Key Behaviors:**
- NIHSS codes (R29.7-) can be used in conjunction with acute stroke codes (I60-I63) to identify neurological status and stroke severity.
- Stroke scale codes must be sequenced after the acute stroke diagnosis code(s).
- Facilities must report the initial score documented at a minimum.
- Facilities may choose to capture multiple stroke scale scores if desired.
- NIHSS stroke scale documentation may be provided by clinicians other than the patient's provider.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- NIHSS codes must be sequenced after the acute stroke diagnosis code(s).
- At least one initial NIHSS score must be reported.
