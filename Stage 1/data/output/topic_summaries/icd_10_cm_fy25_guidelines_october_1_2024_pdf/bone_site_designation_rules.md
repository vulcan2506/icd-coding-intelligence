# Bone Site Designation Rules
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Bone Site Designation Rules

**Key Behaviors:**
- Conditions affecting the upper or lower end of a bone must be coded to the bone site, not the joint.
- Even if the affected portion is at the joint, the site designation remains the bone.
- Examples include avascular necrosis (M87), Osteoporosis (M80), and Osteoporosis (M81).

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The condition must be documented as affecting the bone (e.g., avascular necrosis, osteoporosis).
- The specific location (upper or lower end) must be identifiable or implied by the condition type.
