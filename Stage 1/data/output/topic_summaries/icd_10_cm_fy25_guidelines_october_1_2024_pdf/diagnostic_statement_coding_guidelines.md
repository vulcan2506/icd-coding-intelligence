# Diagnostic Statement Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Diagnostic Statement Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code diagnoses included in the final diagnostic statement (e.g., discharge summary, face sheet) unless they are resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions with no bearing on the current stay.
- Do not report resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions that have no impact on the current hospital stay.
- Use history codes (categories Z80-Z87) as secondary codes only if the historical condition or family history has an impact on current care or influences treatment.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Diagnosis must be included in the final diagnostic statement to be ordinarily coded.
- Resolved conditions or status-post procedures must have a bearing on the current stay to be reported.
- History codes require documented impact on current care or treatment influence to be valid as secondary codes.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Coding resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions that have no bearing on the current stay.

**New in this version:**
- Explicit permission to use history codes (Z80-Z87) as secondary codes when the historical condition impacts current care.
