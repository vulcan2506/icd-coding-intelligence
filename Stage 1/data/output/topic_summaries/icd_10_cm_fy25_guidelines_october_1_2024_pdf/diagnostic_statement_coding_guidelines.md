# Diagnostic Statement Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Coding of Diagnostic Statements and History

**Key Behaviors:**
- Diagnoses included in the final diagnostic statement (e.g., discharge summary, face sheet) should ordinarily be coded.
- Resolved conditions or status-post procedures from previous admissions with no bearing on the current stay are not to be reported.
- History codes (categories Z80-Z87) may be used as secondary codes if the historical condition or family history impacts current care or influences treatment.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The diagnosis must be included in the final diagnostic statement such as the discharge summary or face sheet.
- The historical condition must have an impact on current care or influence treatment to justify using a history code.
