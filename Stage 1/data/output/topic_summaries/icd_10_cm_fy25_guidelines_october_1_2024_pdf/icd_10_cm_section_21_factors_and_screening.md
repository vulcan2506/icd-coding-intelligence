# ICD-10-CM Section 21 Factors and Screening
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Section 21 Factors and Screening

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code factors influencing health status and contact with health services when documented as the reason for encounter
- Assign screening codes when a patient undergoes a screening test or procedure for a specific condition

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be documented as a factor influencing health status or contact with health services
- Screening must be performed for a specific condition and documented as such
