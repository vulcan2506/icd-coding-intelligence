# Secondary Malignant Neoplasm Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary Malignant Neoplasm Coding Logic

**Key Behaviors:**
- Use category Z85 (Personal history of malignant neoplasm) when the primary site is excised/eradicated with no further treatment or evidence of existing primary malignancy
- Code any mention of extension, invasion, or metastasis to another site as a secondary malignant neoplasm to that site
- The secondary site may be assigned as the principal or first-listed diagnosis
- The Z85 code is used as a secondary code when the secondary site is the principal diagnosis
- Refer to section I.C.2.t for specific coding of secondary malignant neoplasm of lymphoid tissue

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must confirm the primary malignancy has been excised or eradicated
- Documentation must confirm there is no further treatment directed to the former primary site
- Documentation must confirm there is no evidence of an existing primary malignancy at the former site
