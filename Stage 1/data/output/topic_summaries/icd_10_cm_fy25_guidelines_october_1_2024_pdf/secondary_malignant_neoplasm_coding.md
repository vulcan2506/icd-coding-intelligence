# Secondary Malignant Neoplasm Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Secondary Malignant Neoplasm Coding Logic

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes from category Z85 (Personal history of malignant neoplasm) are used when a primary malignancy has been excised or eradicated with no further treatment or evidence at that site
- Any mention of extension, invasion, or metastasis to another site is coded as a secondary malignant neoplasm to that site
- The secondary site may be assigned as the principal or first-listed diagnosis
- The Z85 code is used as a secondary code to indicate the former site of the malignancy
- Coding rules apply specifically to secondary malignant neoplasm of lymphoid tissue as referenced in section I.C.2.t
