# Section 14 Psychosocial Hazard Documentation
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Section 14 Psychosocial Hazard Documentation

**Key Behaviors:**
- Clinicians other than the patient's provider must document psychosocial hazards related to socioeconomic circumstances.
- Documentation must align with ICD-10-CM categories Z55-Z65 for potential health hazards.
- Providers must identify and record external causes linked to socioeconomic factors affecting patient health.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must be provided by clinicians other than the patient's primary provider.
- The hazard must be directly related to socioeconomic or psychosocial circumstances.
- The condition must fall within the Z55-Z65 code range for potential health hazards.

**New in this version:**
- Mandatory inclusion of external cause codes for psychosocial hazards when documented by non-provider clinicians.
