# Section 14 Psychosocial Hazard Documentation
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Section 14 Psychosocial Hazard Documentation

**Key Behaviors:**
- Clinicians other than the patient's provider must document psychosocial hazards related to socioeconomic circumstances.
- Documentation must reference Section I.B.14 for codes Z55-Z65.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Documentation must be provided by clinicians other than the patient's provider.
- Condition must relate to socioeconomic and psychosocial circumstances.
