# Pediatric Condition Coding Guideline
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Pediatric Condition Coding Guideline

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign codes for conditions specified by the provider as having implications for future health care needs.
- This guideline should not be used for adult patients.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be specified by the provider as having implications for future health care needs.
- Patient must be pediatric (not adult).
