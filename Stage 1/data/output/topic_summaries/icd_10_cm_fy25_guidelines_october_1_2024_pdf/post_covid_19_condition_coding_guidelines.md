# Post-COVID-19 Condition Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Post-COVID-19 Condition Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign code U07.1 and J80 for acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS) due to COVID-19.
- Assign specific symptom/condition codes plus U09.9 for sequela of COVID-19 or associated conditions developing after a previous infection.
- Do not assign U09.9 for manifestations of an active (current) COVID-19 infection.
- Assign U09.9 in conjunction with U07.1 if a patient has conditions from a previous infection and develops a new active infection.
- Assign codes for specific conditions associated with the previous infection and codes for manifestations of the new active infection when both are present.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Condition must be documented as sequela or associated symptoms developing following a previous COVID-19 infection.
- Specific symptom(s) or condition(s) related to the previous infection must be known.
- Active (current) COVID-19 infection must be ruled out for U09.9 assignment.
- Both previous infection-related conditions and new active infection must be documented to assign U09.9 with U07.1.
