# Post-COVID-19 Condition Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Post-COVID-19 Condition Coding Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign codes U07.1 and J80 for acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS) due to COVID-19.
- Assign specific symptom codes plus U09.9 for sequela or conditions developing after a previous COVID-19 infection.
- Do not assign U09.9 for manifestations of an active (current) COVID-19 infection.
- Assign U09.9 with U07.1 if a patient has post-COVID conditions and develops a new active infection.
- Code specific conditions associated with previous infection alongside manifestations of the new active infection.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must confirm the condition is a sequela or developed following a previous infection to use U09.9.
- Active (current) COVID-19 infection must be ruled out before assigning U09.9 for its manifestations.
- Specific symptoms or conditions related to the previous infection must be identified and coded individually.
- Both post-COVID condition codes and active infection codes must be assigned when both conditions coexist.
- ARDS due to COVID-19 requires assignment of both U07.1 and J80.

**Deprecated in this version:**
- Assigning U09.9 for manifestations of an active (current) COVID-19 infection.
- Coding only U09.9 without specific symptom codes for post-COVID conditions.
- Failing to assign U09.9 when a patient has conditions associated with a previous infection and a new active infection.

**New in this version:**
- Introduction of code U09.9 for Post COVID-19 condition, unspecified.
- Requirement to code specific symptoms/conditions alongside U09.9 for sequela.
- Guideline allowing concurrent assignment of U09.9 and U07.1 for mixed active and post-COVID cases.
- Mandatory assignment of J80 alongside U07.1 for ARDS due to COVID-19.
