# ICD-10 Category M81 Osteoporosis Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Category M81 Osteoporosis Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Site is not a component of codes under category M81 for osteoporosis without current pathological fracture.
- Site codes under category M80 identify the fracture site, not the osteoporosis itself.
- Category M81 is used for patients with osteoporosis who do not currently have a pathologic fracture due to the condition, even if they have had a fracture in the past.
- Status code Z87.310 (Personal history of osteoporosis fracture) should follow the M81 code for patients with a history of osteoporosis fractures.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Confirm the patient does not currently have a pathologic fracture due to osteoporosis before assigning category M81.
- If a history of osteoporosis fracture exists, append status code Z87.310 after the primary M81 code.
- Do not assign site-specific codes to the osteoporosis diagnosis itself under category M81.
