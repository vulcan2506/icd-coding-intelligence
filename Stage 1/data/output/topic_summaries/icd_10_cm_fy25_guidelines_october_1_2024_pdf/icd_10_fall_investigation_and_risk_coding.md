# ICD-10 Fall Investigation and Risk Coding
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Fall Investigation and Risk Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code R29.6 for encounters where a patient has recently fallen and the reason is being investigated.
- Code Z91.81 for patients who have fallen in the past and are at risk for future falls.
- Both codes R29.6 and Z91.81 may be assigned together when appropriate.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- R29.6 requires the encounter to be for investigating the reason for a recent fall.
- Z91.81 requires documentation that the patient has a history of falling and is at risk for future falls.
