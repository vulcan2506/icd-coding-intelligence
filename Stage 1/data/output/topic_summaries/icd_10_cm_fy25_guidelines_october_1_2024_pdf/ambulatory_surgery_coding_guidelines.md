# Ambulatory Surgery Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Ambulatory Surgery Diagnosis Selection

**Key Behaviors:**
- Code the diagnosis for which the surgery was performed.
- Select the postoperative diagnosis if it is known to be different from the preoperative diagnosis at the time of confirmation.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- The postoperative diagnosis must be confirmed at the time of coding.
- The postoperative diagnosis must be different from the preoperative diagnosis to trigger selection.
