# ICD-10-CM Chapter Usage Restrictions
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10-CM Chapter Usage Restrictions

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes in this chapter are never for use on the maternal record.
- Codes from Chapter 15, the obstetric chapter, are never permitted on the newborn record.
- Chapter 16 codes may be used throughout the life of the patient if the condition is still present.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Maternal record must not contain codes from the chapter in question.
- Newborn record must not contain codes from Chapter 15 (obstetric chapter).
- Chapter 16 codes require the condition to be still present to be used.
