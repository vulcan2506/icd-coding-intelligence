# ICD-10 Maternal Fetal Care Coding Rules
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** ICD-10 Maternal Fetal Care Coding Rules

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign 7th character '0' for single gestations or when documentation is insufficient to determine the affected fetus.
- Codes from categories O35 and O36 are assigned only when the fetal condition modifies maternal management (e.g., requiring diagnostic studies or special care).
- The existence of a fetal condition alone does not justify assigning a code from O35 or O36 to the mother's record.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- 7th character must be assigned for categories O31, O32, O33.3-O33.6, O35, O36, O40, O41, O60.1, O60.2, O64, and O69.
- 7th character '0' is mandatory when it is not clinically possible to determine which fetus is affected.
- Fetal condition must be the direct cause of modified maternal management to validate O35/O36 codes.
