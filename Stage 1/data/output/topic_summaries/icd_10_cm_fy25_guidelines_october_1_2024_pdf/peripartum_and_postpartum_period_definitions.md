# Peripartum and Postpartum Period Definitions
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Peripartum and Postpartum Period Definitions

**Key Behaviors:**
- The postpartum period begins immediately after delivery and continues for six weeks following delivery.
- The peripartum period is defined as the last month of pregnancy to five months postpartum.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Documentation must specify the time frame relative to delivery to distinguish between peripartum and postpartum periods.
- Codes must align with the specific duration defined for the condition (e.g., within 6 weeks for postpartum, within 5 months postpartum for peripartum).
