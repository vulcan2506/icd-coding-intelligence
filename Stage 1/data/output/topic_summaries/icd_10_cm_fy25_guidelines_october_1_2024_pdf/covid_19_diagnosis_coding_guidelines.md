# COVID-19 Diagnosis Coding Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** COVID-19 Symptom and Exposure Coding

**Key Behaviors:**
- Assign codes for specific signs and symptoms (e.g., acute cough, shortness of breath, fever) when a definitive COVID-19 diagnosis is not established.
- Assign Z20.822 as an additional code for patients with signs/symptoms who have an actual or suspected contact with or exposure to COVID-19.
- Consider asymptomatic individuals who test positive for COVID-19 as having the infection and assign the appropriate code.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- Definitive diagnosis must not be established to use symptom codes (R05.1, R05.9, R06.02, R50.9).
- Contact with or exposure to COVID-19 must be documented to assign Z20.822.
- Positive test result must be documented for asymptomatic individuals to confirm infection status.
