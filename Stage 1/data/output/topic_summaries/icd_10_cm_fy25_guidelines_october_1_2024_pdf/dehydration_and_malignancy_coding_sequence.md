# Dehydration and Malignancy Coding Sequence
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Dehydration and Malignancy Coding Sequence

**Key Behaviors:**
- When the admission is for management of dehydration due to malignancy and only dehydration is being treated, sequence dehydration first
- Follow the dehydration code with the code(s) for the malignancy

**Requirements / Properties:**
- Admission or encounter must be specifically for management of dehydration
- Dehydration must be caused by the malignancy
- Only dehydration is being treated (e.g., intravenous rehydration)
- No other active treatment for the malignancy is occurring during this encounter
