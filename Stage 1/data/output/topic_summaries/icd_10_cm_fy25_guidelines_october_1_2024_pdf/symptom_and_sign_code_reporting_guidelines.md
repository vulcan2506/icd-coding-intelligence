# Symptom and Sign Code Reporting Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Symptom and Sign Code Reporting Guidelines

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes describing symptoms and signs are acceptable for reporting when a related definitive diagnosis has not been established by the provider.
- Chapter 18 of ICD-10-CM (codes R00-R99) contains many, but not all, codes for symptoms.
- Symptom codes should be used as opposed to diagnoses when the diagnosis remains unconfirmed.

**Requirements / Properties:**
- A related definitive diagnosis must not be established (confirmed) by the provider for symptom codes to be acceptable.
- The provider must not have confirmed the diagnosis prior to coding.
