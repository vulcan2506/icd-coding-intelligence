# Symptom and Sign Code Reporting Guidelines
*Source: ICD-10-CM FY25 Guidelines October 1, 2024.pdf*

**Feature:** Symptom and Sign Code Reporting

**Key Behaviors:**
- Codes describing symptoms and signs are acceptable for reporting when a related definitive diagnosis has not been established by the provider.
- Codes describing symptoms and signs are acceptable for reporting when a diagnosis has not been confirmed by the provider.
- Chapter 18 (R00-R99) contains many, but not all, codes for symptoms.

**Mandatory Coding Criteria:**
- A related definitive diagnosis must not be established (confirmed) by the provider.
- The condition must be documented as a symptom or sign rather than a confirmed diagnosis.
